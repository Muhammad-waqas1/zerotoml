# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

ZeroToML is a learning resource, and learning requires psychological safety. Everyone who participates in this project — contributors, issue reporters, and discussion participants — should feel welcome regardless of age, background, education level, experience, gender identity, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behaviour:**

- Be kind, patient, and constructive in all interactions
- Assume good intent — most mistakes are honest, not malicious
- Give and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Credit others' contributions
- Welcome newcomers — everyone was a beginner once

**Unacceptable behaviour:**

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling, deliberately inflammatory comments, or off-topic political debates
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and any other channel used by this project's community.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainer at [GitHub](https://github.com/Muhammad-waqas1). All reports will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, or to temporarily or permanently ban contributors for other behaviours they deem inappropriate.
